Welcome to RPI Acacia's Recruitment Home page!

Being in a fraternity is more than about partying; a good fraternity is a tight-knit group of college men dedicated to their studies, each other, and their community. This is an important value of Acacia's called the Order of Priorities. To learn more about Acacia's values, history, and more, you can visit Acacia's International site, or for more chapter specific information, the Rensselaer Acacia official site.

It can be fun, too, though. Check out the events we have planned for Spring 2015, or see a full-size calendar. You can also recieve a printout if you see us at Meet the Greeks. In addition, view some of our rush photos from previous years.
